--- layout: recipe title: Trademark Margarita permalink: /recipes/trademark-margarita.html --- # Trademark Margarita ## Ingredients - 1.5 oz blanco tequila - 0.5 oz Cointreau - 0.75 oz lemon juice - 0.75 oz lime juice - 0.75 oz simple syrup ## Instructions 1. Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice. 2. Shake hard until well chilled. 3. Strain into a glass and serve. ## Notes
