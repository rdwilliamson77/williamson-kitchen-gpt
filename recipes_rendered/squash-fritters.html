--- layout: recipe title: Squash Fritters permalink: /recipes/squash-fritters.html --- # Squash Fritters ## Ingredients - 2 eggs, beaten - 4 green onions, chopped fine - 1 teaspoon salt - 3 tablespoons flour - 3 tablespoons cornmeal - 2 teaspoons melted butter - 3 cups grated raw squash - 2 teaspoons sugar - pepper, to taste ## Instructions 1. Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl until well combined. 2. Drop by spoonfuls onto a griddle or skillet sprayed with Pam (or lightly greased). 3. Cook like pancakes, turning once, until golden brown on both sides. ## Notes
