# Katie’s Cheesecake ## Ingredients - crust - filling - topping ## Instructions 1. Crush Holland Rusks and stir into the melted margarine. 2. Press into springform pan. (Crust can also be made with graham cracker crumbs and margarine.) 3. Mix 4 eggs on high speed. Gradually add 1 1/4 cups sugar. 4. Break cream cheese into chunks and add a little at a time, beating on high speed a total of 15 minutes. 5. Pour into springform pan and bake at 350°F for 40 minutes. 6. Mix sour cream with 1/2 cup sugar and vanilla. 7. When cheesecake has cooled for 10 minutes, pour sour cream mixture gently over top. 8. Return to oven for an additional 10 minutes. 9. Chill all day or overnight before serving. ## Notes
