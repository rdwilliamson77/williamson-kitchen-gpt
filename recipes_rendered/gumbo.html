--- layout: recipe title: Gumbo permalink: /recipes/gumbo.html --- # Gumbo ## Ingredients - 1/4 cup + 1 tablespoon bacon drippings - 1/4 cup + 2 tablespoons flour - 2 medium onions, chopped - 1 1/2 cups celery, chopped - 1 clove garlic, minced - 1 (28 oz) can whole tomatoes - 1 (16 oz) can tomato sauce - 3 cups water - 3 cups chicken broth - 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce - 1 tablespoon salt - 1 teaspoon pepper - 2 pounds shrimp, cleaned - 1 1/2 pounds crab meat - 1 (16 oz) package okra - File powder, to taste - Tabasco sauce (optional) ## Instructions 1. Combine bacon drippings and flour in a large pot over medium heat. Stir constantly until roux is dark brown. This can take time. 2. {'(Optional shortcut': 'Tom recommends spreading the flour on a baking sheet and browning in a 450Â°F oven, stirring frequently until browned. Then combine with bacon drippings in pot.)'} 3. Stir in onions, celery, and garlic. Cook for about 5 minutes, or until onions are clear. 4. Add whole tomatoes, tomato sauce, water, broth, salt, and pepper. Simmer over medium heat for 1 hour. 5. Add shrimp, crab meat, and okra. Simmer 20 minutes. 6. Stir in Worcestershire sauce and simmer another 10 minutes. 7. Add file powder and Tabasco to taste.
