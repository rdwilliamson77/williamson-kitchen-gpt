--- layout: recipe title: Poppy Seed Chicken permalink: /recipes/poppyseed-chicken.html --- # Poppy Seed Chicken ## Ingredients - 3 or 4 boneless chicken breasts - 1 cup lowfat cream of chicken soup - 1 cup light sour cream - 1 row Ritz crackers, crushed - 1/2 cup lowfat margarine - 1/4 cup poppy seed - white rice, enough for 4 servings ## Instructions 1. Cook chicken breasts in water seasoned with salt and 2 chicken bouillon cubes. 2. Boil slowly for 45 minutes, covered, then cool and cut into small pieces. 3. Place chicken in a small baking dish. 4. In a separate bowl, combine sour cream and cream of chicken soup. Spread over chicken. 5. Crush Ritz crackers and mix with melted margarine. 6. Sprinkle cracker mixture over the chicken, then sprinkle with poppy seed. 7. Bake at 350Â°F for 20 to 30 minutes. 8. Serve over steamed white rice. ## Notes
