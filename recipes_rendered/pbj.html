# Classic Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich ## Ingredients - 2 slices sandwich bread (white, wheat, or sourdough) - 2 tablespoons peanut butter (creamy or chunky) - 2 tablespoons fruit jelly or jam (grape, strawberry, or your choice) ## Instructions 1. Lay both slices of bread flat on a cutting board. 2. Spread peanut butter evenly over one slice. 3. Spread jelly evenly over the other slice. 4. Press the two slices together, spreads facing each other. 5. Cut diagonally or down the middle, if desired. ## Notes - Use toasted bread for extra texture. - Try mixing different nut butters or jams for variety. - This can be wrapped in wax paper for lunchboxes or road trips.
