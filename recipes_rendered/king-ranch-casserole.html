--- layout: recipe title: King Ranch Casserole permalink: /recipes/king-ranch-casserole.html --- # King Ranch Casserole ## Ingredients - 4 chicken breasts - 3 tablespoons vegetable oil - 1 chopped onion - 1 chopped green pepper - 1 can Rotel tomatoes - 1 teaspoon chili powder - 1 teaspoon cumin - 1 teaspoon oregano - 1 teaspoon salt - 1/2 teaspoon pepper - 1 can cream of chicken soup - 1 can cream of mushroom soup - 1 cup chicken broth - 1 package (8-inch) corn tortillas - 1 cup grated cheddar cheese ## Instructions 1. Boil chicken in seasoned water, covered, for about 45 minutes or until tender. 2. Remove chicken from bone, chop into bite-sized pieces, and reserve broth. 3. In a large skillet, heat oil and sauté onion and green pepper until tender. 4. Add Rotel tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine. 5. In a separate bowl, mix the soups and chicken broth, then stir into the skillet mixture. 6. In a 10x16-inch baking dish, layer chopped chicken on the bottom. 7. Cut tortillas into fourths and add a layer on top of the chicken. 8. Pour half the sauce mixture over the tortillas. 9. Repeat the layers of chicken, tortillas, and sauce. 10. Top with grated cheddar cheese. 11. Cover with foil and bake at 350°F for 25–30 minutes. ## Notes
