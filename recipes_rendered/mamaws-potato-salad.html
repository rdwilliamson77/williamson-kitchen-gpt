# Mamaw’s Potato Salad ## Ingredients - 8 large red potatoes - 4 hard boiled eggs - 1/2 cup chopped dill pickle - 1/2 cup diced celery - 1 cup mayonnaise - 1/4 cup mustard - 1/4 cup dill pickle juice - Lawry’s Seasoning Salt, to taste ## Instructions 1. Boil unpeeled red potatoes in a large pot of salted water until tender, about 30 minutes. 2. Drain, cool, and peel the potatoes. 3. Cut into small squares and place in a large serving bowl with chopped eggs, pickles, and celery. 4. Sprinkle generously with Lawry’s Seasoning Salt. 5. Mix together mayonnaise, mustard, and pickle juice in a small bowl. 6. Pour dressing over the potato mixture and stir gently to combine. 7. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve. ## Notes
