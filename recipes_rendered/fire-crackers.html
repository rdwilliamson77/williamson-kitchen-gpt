--- layout: recipe title: Fire Crackers permalink: /recipes/fire-crackers.html --- # Fire Crackers ## Ingredients - 1 box (16 oz) saltine crackers (about 4 sleeves) - 1 cup canola oil (or another neutral oil) - 1 oz packet dry ranch seasoning mix - 2 tablespoons crushed red pepper flakes - 1 teaspoon garlic powder (optional) - 1 teaspoon onion powder (optional) ## Instructions 1. In a large mixing bowl or zip-top bag, combine the canola oil, ranch seasoning, crushed red pepper, and optional garlic/onion powder. 2. Stir or shake vigorously until the mixture is fully emulsified. 3. Add the crackers and gently turn or shake until every cracker is evenly coated. 4. Transfer to a large airtight container or gallon zip-top bag. 5. Let sit for at least 12 hours (24 is better), gently turning occasionally to redistribute the seasoning. 6. Serve as-is or with sliced cheese, pimento cheese, or cold beer. ## Notes - - - - T - h - e - s - e - - s - t - o - r - e - - w - e - l - l - - f - o - r - - u - p - - t - o - - a - - w - e - e - k - - i - n - - a - n - - a - i - r - t - i - g - h - t - - c - o - n - t - a - i - n - e - r - . - - - - - T - h - e - - s - p - i - c - e - - l - e - v - e - l - - c - a - n - - b - e - - a - d - j - u - s - t - e - d - - â€” - - 1 - - t - b - s - p - - r - e - d - - p - e - p - p - e - r - - m - a - k - e - s - - t - h - e - m - - m - i - l - d - e - r - , - - 2 - - t - b - s - p - - i - s - - c - l - a - s - s - i - c - . -
