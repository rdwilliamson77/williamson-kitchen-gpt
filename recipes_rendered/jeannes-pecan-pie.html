# Jeanne’s Pecan Pie ## Ingredients - 3 eggs - 2/3 cup sugar - 3/4 cup maple Karo syrup - 1/2 cup melted butter - 1 teaspoon vanilla - 1 cup pecans ## Instructions 1. Preheat oven to 400°F. 2. Beat the eggs in a large mixing bowl. 3. Add sugar, Karo syrup, melted butter, and vanilla; mix well. 4. Stir in pecans. 5. Pour mixture into an unbaked pie shell. 6. Bake at 400°F for 10 minutes. 7. Reduce heat to 325°F and continue baking for 25 to 35 minutes, or until set. ## Notes
