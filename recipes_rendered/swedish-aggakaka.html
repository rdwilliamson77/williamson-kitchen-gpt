--- layout: recipe title: Swedish Äggakaka (Egg Cake) permalink: /recipes/swedish-aggakaka.html --- # Swedish Äggakaka (Egg Cake) ## Ingredients - 1/4 cup butter, melted - 5 eggs - 3 cups milk - 1/8 cup sugar - 1 teaspoon salt - 1 1/2 cups flour - powdered sugar (for serving) - lemon juice (for serving) - preserves (for serving) ## Instructions 1. Preheat oven to 450°F. 2. Melt butter in a 9x13 glass baking dish. 3. In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs, then blend in milk, sugar, salt, and flour until smooth. 4. Pour batter over the melted butter in the hot dish. 5. Bake for 20 minutes until puffed and golden. 6. Once it falls, cut into squares and serve with either preserves or powdered sugar with a drizzle of lemon juice. ## Notes
