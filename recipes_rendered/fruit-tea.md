ingredients:
- 1 gallon brewed tea
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 can orange juice
- 1 can lemonade
- 12 oz pineapple juice
instructions:
- Brew one gallon of tea and allow to cool slightly.
- Stir in sugar while tea is still warm to dissolve completely.
- Add orange juice, lemonade, and pineapple juice.
- Mix well and chill.
- Serve over ice.
