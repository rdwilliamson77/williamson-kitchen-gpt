--- layout: recipe title: Pumpkin Roll permalink: /recipes/pumpkin-roll.html --- # Pumpkin Roll ## Ingredients - cake - filling ## Instructions 1. Beat eggs for 5 minutes at high speed. 2. Gradually add sugar and stir in pumpkin, lemon juice, flour, baking powder, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, and salt. 3. Spread batter in a greased 15 x 10 x 1 inch jelly roll pan. Top with chopped pecans. 4. Bake at 375Â°F for 15 minutes. 5. Loosen edges and turn out onto a kitchen towel. Sprinkle with powdered sugar. 6. Roll up towel and cake together from narrow end. Let cool completely. 7. For the filling, beat powdered sugar, cream cheese, butter, and vanilla until smooth. 8. Unroll cake and spread filling evenly over the surface. 9. Roll cake back up (without the towel) and wrap in foil. Chill before serving. ## Notes
