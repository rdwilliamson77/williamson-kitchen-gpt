# Sesame Chicken ## Ingredients - 4 chicken breasts, cut into pieces - 1/2 cup evaporated milk (Pet milk) - 1 beaten egg - 1 1/2 cups flour - 1/4 teaspoon baking powder - 1 1/2 teaspoons salt - 1/2 teaspoon thyme - 2 teaspoons paprika - 1/2 teaspoon pepper - 1/2 cup melted margarine - 1/2 cup sesame seeds ## Instructions 1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. 2. Melt margarine on a cookie sheet. 3. In a bowl, combine evaporated milk and beaten egg. 4. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, thyme, paprika, and pepper. 5. Dip chicken pieces in the milk mixture, then coat in the flour mixture. 6. Place chicken pieces on the cookie sheet, coating both sides in the melted margarine. 7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds. 8. Bake for 30 minutes until golden and cooked through. ## Notes
