--- layout: recipe title: Almond Tea Punch permalink: /recipes/almond-tea-punch.html --- # Almond Tea Punch ## Ingredients - {'item': '1/2 cup lemon juice'} - {'item': '2 cups sugar'} - {'item': '1 quart water'} - {'item': '2 cups strong tea'} - {'item': '1 tablespoon vanilla'} - {'item': '2 tablespoons almond extract'} - {'item': '2 quarts ginger ale'} ## Instructions 1. Boil 2 cups of water and steep a family-sized tea bag in it for 15 minutes, covered. 2. Add sugar, lemon juice, vanilla, and almond extract to the tea and mix thoroughly until sugar is dissolved. 3. Add another quart of water. Refrigerate until ready to serve. 4. Add ginger ale just before serving. Serve over ice. ## Notes
