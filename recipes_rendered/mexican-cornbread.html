# Mexican Cornbread ## Ingredients - 1 cup cornmeal - 1 teaspoon salt - 1/2 teaspoon baking soda - 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder or 2 cloves minced fresh garlic - 1 small chopped onion - 1 cup buttermilk - 1/3 cup vegetable oil - 2 eggs - 1 small can creamed corn - 1/2 pound grated cheddar cheese - 1 to 3 tablespoons chopped jalapeño peppers (depending on desired heat) ## Instructions 1. Preheat oven to 375°F and grease and heat an 8x8-inch baking pan. 2. Sift together cornmeal, salt, soda, and garlic powder in a large bowl. 3. In a separate bowl, beat eggs slightly, then mix with buttermilk and creamed corn. 4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry mixture. 5. Stir in onion, vegetable oil, cheese, and jalapeños. 6. Pour batter into the prepared hot pan. 7. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, or until golden and set in the center. ## Notes
