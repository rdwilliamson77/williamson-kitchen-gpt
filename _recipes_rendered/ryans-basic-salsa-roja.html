# Ryan’s Basic Salsa Roja ## Ingredients - 2–3 lbs ripe Roma tomatoes, halved - 1 large onion, halved - 4–6 garlic cloves, unpeeled - 2–3 serrano peppers - 1 small fresh tomato, diced - 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped - Juice of 1–2 limes - Salt, to taste - Pepper, to taste ## Instructions 1. Preheat broiler. 2. Place Roma tomato halves skin-side up on a sheet pan with onion, garlic, and peppers. 3. Broil for 8–10 minutes, or until skins blister and blacken. 4. Remove from oven and allow to cool slightly. 5. Peel garlic; remove pepper stems. 6. Blend charred vegetables in a food processor along with the fresh diced tomato. 7. Add lime juice, salt, pepper, and cilantro. Pulse until just combined, leaving some texture. 8. Strain excess liquid for a thicker consistency, if desired. ## Notes - T - h - i - s - - s - a - l - s - a - - h - o - l - d - s - - w - e - l - l - - i - n - - t - h - e - - r - e - f - r - i - g - e - r - a - t - o - r - - f - o - r - - u - p - - t - o - - 5 - - d - a - y - s - . - - I - t - - i - m - p - r - o - v - e - s - - i - n - - f - l - a - v - o - r - - o - v - e - r - n - i - g - h - t - . - - A - l - w - a - y - s - - t - a - s - t - e - - f - o - r - - s - e - a - s - o - n - i - n - g - - b - e - f - o - r - e - - s - e - r - v - i - n - g - , - - a - s - - a - c - i - d - i - t - y - - a - n - d - - h - e - a - t - - c - a - n - - m - e - l - l - o - w - - o - v - e - r - - t - i - m - e - . -
