# Fruit Tea ## Ingredients - 1 gallon brewed tea - 2 cups sugar - 1 can orange juice - 1 can lemonade - 12 oz pineapple juice ## Instructions 1. Brew one gallon of tea and allow to cool slightly. 2. Stir in sugar while tea is still warm to dissolve completely. 3. Add orange juice, lemonade, and pineapple juice. 4. Mix well and chill. 5. Serve over ice. ## Notes
