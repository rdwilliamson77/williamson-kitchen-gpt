# Apple Salad ## Ingredients - 3 to 4 Red Delicious apples, cut into bite-size pieces - 1 can chunk pineapple, drained - 1 cup pecans - 3 to 4 pieces American cheese, torn - 1/2 cup sugar - 2 tablespoons flour - Juice from can of pineapples - 1 beaten egg ## Instructions 1. {'Toss together in bowl': 'apples, pineapple, pecans, cheese.'} 2. Make sauce: cook in small pan till thick (stirring constantly): 1/2 cup sugar, 2 tbsp flour, juice from can of pineapples, 1 beaten egg. 3. Pour over apple mixture while hot. 4. Refrigerate. ## Notes
